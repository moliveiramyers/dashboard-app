1. installed pnpm (package manager)
2. Create app file: npx create-next-app@latest nextjs-dashboard --example "https://github.com/vercel/next-learn/tree/main/dashboard/starter-example" --use-pnpm
3. pnpm approve-builds --all
4. pnpm i
5. pnpm dev
6. Auth: pnpm i next-auth@beta
7. Secret key generation: npx auth secret
